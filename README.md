# Hi, I'm Joan Caceres

I am a research scientist working at the intersection of superconducting quantum circuits, hybrid quantum devices, quantum information, and scientific computing.

I use this space to share research-oriented tools, simulations, and utilities that support workflows in quantum computing and superconducting circuit design.

## What I Work On

- Hybrid and superconducting quantum circuits
- Quantum information and quantum computing
- Circuit simulation, modeling, and analysis tools
- Quantum hardware design workflows
- Layout, CAD, and automation utilities for research

## Featured Projects

- [cqedraw](https://github.com/joanjcaceres/cqedraw) - Browser and desktop GUI for drawing superconducting circuit graphs and generating matrix snippets for Black Box Quantization workflows.
- [HybridSuperQubits](https://github.com/joanjcaceres/HybridSuperQubits) - Python framework for simulating hybrid semiconductor-superconductor quantum circuits.
- [klayout-duplication-cleaner](https://github.com/joanjcaceres/klayout-duplication-cleaner) - Scripts for detecting and removing duplicate shapes in KLayout layouts.
